Brands / Developers

We thank the developers for doing a splendid job and giving gamers their best experience!

  • ROCKSTAR GAMES


    Rockstar Games, Inc. is an American video game publisher based in New York City. The company was established in December 1998 as a subsidiary of Take-Two Interactive, using the assets Take-Two had previously acquired from BMG Interactive

  • Activision


    Activision Publishing, Inc. is an American video game publisher based in Santa Monica, California. It currently serves as the publishing business for its parent company, Activision Blizzard, and consists of several subsidiary studios

  • EA sports


    EA Sports is a division of Electronic Arts that develops and publishes sports video games. Formerly a marketing gimmick of Electronic Arts, in which they tried to imitate real-life sports networks

  • UBSOFT


    Ubisoft Entertainment SA is a French video game company headquartered in Montreuil with several development studios across the world. Its video game franchises include Rayman, Raving Rabbids, Prince of Persia, Assassin's Creed, Far Cry, Watch Dogs, Just Dance, and the Tom Clancy's series.
